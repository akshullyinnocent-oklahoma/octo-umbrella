FEBRUARY 4TH 2026

ORDER DENYING MOTION TO DISQUALIFY JUDGE THAD BALKMAN

Petitioner was sentenced after Jury Trial to two consecutive terms of 25 years on August 31, 2022, by this Court. Petitioner has filed an Application for Post Conviction Relief, which is pending before this Court. Petitioner has also filed Motions to Disqualify the prosecutors and judges involved in this case.

In his Motion to Disqualify Judge Thad Balkman, Petitioner alleges five specific judicial errors and claims disqualification should follow. Petitioner cites Rule 2.11(A) of the Oklahoma Code of Judicial Conduct as the basis for disqualification.

All of Petitioner's complaints about the judge are also brought up in his Application for Post Conviction Relief. Other than bare allegations, he fails to cite to any record or substantiate in any way that the judge acted impartially or
demonstrated the appearance of bias or conflict of interest in violation of Rule 2.11(A)
of the Oklahoma Code of Judicial Conduct. 

Furthermore, the Legislature has granted
to the trial court the jurisdiction to consider Post Conviction matters.

Signed
Thad Balkman
