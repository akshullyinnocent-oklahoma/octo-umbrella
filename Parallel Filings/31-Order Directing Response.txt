ORDER DIRECTING RESPONSE

Petitioner, appearing pro se, has filed with the Clerk of this Court a Petition for Mandamus Relief, requesting this Court issue a Writ of Mandamus to the District Court of Cleveland County. Petitioner alleges that as of the date of filing this request, the District Court has failed to rule on Petitioner's Application for Post-Conviction Relief, which Petitioner alleges was filed in the District Court on October 7, 2025, as well as various motions Petitioner alleges were filed on August 11, 2025.

The District Court of Cleveland County, the Honorable Thad Balkman, District Judge, or a designated representative, SHALL determine whether Petitioner has filed the above-referenced pleadings.

· If no such request was filed, the District Court shall advise this Court by memorandum.
· If the District Court has already disposed of Petitioner's request, a certified copy of the disposition orders shall be forwarded to this Court and to Petitioner.
· If the request was filed and disposition has not been made, the District Court shall act upon the request within thirty (30) days from the date of this order and forward a certified copy of the disposition order to this Court and to Petitioner.

IT IS SO ORDERED.

WITNESS MY HAND AND THE SEAL OF THIS COURT this 12th day of January, 2026.
