### **NOTICE UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF TITLE 12 O.S. 1998 § 2803.1 OF THE STATE’S INTENT TO USE STATEMENTS OF CHILDREN 13 YEARS OR YOUNGER DESCRIBING ACTS OF PHYSICAL OR SEXUAL ABUSE**
COMES NOW, the State of Oklahoma, and hereby notifies the defense of the State’s intention to call the following witnesses at the jury trial set January 24, 2022, at 9:00 a.m., to testify concerning conversations with the victim, a child under 13 years of age, as follows:
 * **Christi Cornett** is employed as a forensic interviewer at the Mary Abbott House and was assigned to the case involving B.J. Mrs. Cornett was informed during an interview on 7/22/19 by B.J. that the defendant touched her genital area with his hands under her underwear. Further, she disclosed that it felt weird and it happened at her grandmother’s house in the bedroom while she was coloring. At the time of the interview, B.J. was 11 years old. She described the incident in detail in her statements to Christi Cornett, as listed in her statement in the Norman Police Department (NPD) reports previously provided to Defendant’s attorney, and the forensic interview DVD provided.
 * **Jeffery Jackson** will testify that B.J. is his daughter. He had a discussion with B.J. about the defendant, where she told him the defendant had touched her inappropriately by touching her private part with his hand and tried to have her touch his penis. He also tried to unhook her bra. At the time of these statements, B.J. was 11 years old. Mr. Jackson will state he relayed this information to B.J.’s mother, the police, and DHS. He will testify consistently with his interview with law enforcement and DHS as detailed in reports given to the defendant.
These statements are admissible under Title 12 O.S. 1998 § 2803.1.
The State herein gives notice of its intent to use all of said evidence in the presentation of the instant case and incorporates these statements into this notice.
