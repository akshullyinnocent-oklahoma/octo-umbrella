**Exhibit M17:**
*   **Description:** A formal printout of **"OCCA Rule 12.9 Petition for Rehearing"** from the Oklahoma Court of Criminal Appeals (OCCA).
*   **Details:**
    *   **A:** The same panel that heard the direct appeal will hear the petition for rehearing, and a decision must be entered within 45 days.
    *   **B:** The petition must be filed within **twenty (20) days** from the date the panel's opinion was filed.
    *   **C:** Petition for rehearing is not filed as a matter of course; it is only permitted for:
        *   (1) A dispositive question of the case duly submitted by the attorney of record has been overlooked by the panel.
        *   (2) The decision is in conflict with an express statute or controlling decision to which the attention of the panel was not called either in the brief or in oral argument.
