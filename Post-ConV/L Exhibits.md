### **Exhibit L Series (Court Transcripts & Index)**

**Exhibit L1:**
*   **Description:** Page 33 of an Official District Court of Oklahoma transcript, focusing on the cross-examination of a forensic interviewer regarding a child's statements.
*   **Details:** 
    *   The witness (likely Christi Cornett) is asked about the spontaneity of the child's initial disclosure. She states she wasn't asking a direct question about what brought the child there; she just wanted to know the child's understanding of the visit.
    *   She confirms the child was clearly told/informed about the meeting's purpose.
    *   When asked if she knew the child had spoken to her father multiple times before the interview, she replies: *"I don't recall her talking multiple times. I knew she had that initial one that prompted that disclosure, but as far as how many times, I don't recall knowing all of that."*
    *   She confirms that it is common for an 11-year-old child to disclose more information in subsequent interviews if the disclosure is spontaneous, and doesn't find it suspicious.
